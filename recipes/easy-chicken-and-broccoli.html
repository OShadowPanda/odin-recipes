Easy Chicken And Broccoli

Chicken and Brocolli Ingredients

This is a cheap chicken breast recipe that's comforting and quick enough for busy evenings.

Ingredients

  • cooking spray

  • 1 pound boneless skinless chicken breasts, cut into chunks

  • 1 (10,5 ounce) can condense cream of mushroom soup

  • 1 1/4 cups water

  • 1 ½ cups instant white rice, uncooked

  • 1 cup Kraft Shredded Three Cheese with a Touch of Philadelphia

Steps

  1. Grease a large skillet with cooking spray and place over medium-hight heat.

    Cook and stir chicken in the hot skillet until browned, 2 to 3 minutes.

  2. Add broccoli, condensed soup, and water;stir. Bring to a boil.

  3. Stir in rice. Top with cheese. Cover and cook over low heat for 5 minutes
