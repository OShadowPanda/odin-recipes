Easy Cheesy chicken and rice

This cheesy rice and chicken is delicious for the whole family!

Ingredients

  • Cooking Spray

  • 1 ½ cups shredded Cheddar cheese, divided

  • 1 teaspoon ground black pepper

  • ½ teaspoon salt

  • 4 skinless, boneless chicken breast halves, or more to taste

  • 2 (10.5 ounce) cans cream of chicken soup

  • 3 cups water, or more as needed

  • 1 (1 ounce) package dry onion soup mix (such as Lipton)

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). Grease a 9x13-inch baking dish with cooking spray.

  2. Spread rice into the bottom of the prepared baking dish. Sprinkle 1 cup Cheddar cheese over rice; season with black pepper and salt. Arrange chicken evenly atop rice-cheese layer. Dot chicken evenly with rounded teaspoons of cream of chicken soup. Pour water over chicken, then sprinkle evenly with onion soup mix.

  3. Bake in the preheated oven for 1 hour. Check rice for doneness; if water is already completely absorbed, add 1/2 cup more water to keep mixture moist. Continue baking until chicken is no longer pink in the center and the juices run clear, about 30 minutes more. An instant-read thermometer inserted into the center should read at least 165 degrees F (74 degrees C).

  4. Sprinkle remaining 1/2 cup Cheddar cheese over the dish to serve.
